Alolan Marowak

About Alolan Marowak

Alolan Marowak was first introduced in the Pokemon Sun and Moon games. He is a fire and ghost type pokemon that can pack a punch! His design is inspired by traditional hawaiian fire dancers.

Alolan Marowak in battle

  1. He has a large bone
  2. He is skilled at using fire type attacks
  3. His signature move is Shadow Bone
